Receiving and Inspection
Used to record and manager the received modules. Application assists in the inspection of the incoming material to determine the acceptance according to the inspection criteria.
Sampling plan:- Method used in QC to determine whether to accept or reject a lot of material
Skip lot:- Inspection technique used in QC where only some of lots in a sequence are inspected rather inspecting each one.

CAPA and SCAR
CAPA/SCAR workflow manages the investigation and elimination of critical and systemic nonconformance using a defined process for problem description, containment, root cause analysis, resolution, and verification of effectiveness.
